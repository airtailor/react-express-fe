    Questions

    • What happens next?

      We’ll send you an order confirmation email after you place your order. It includes order information and a link to view your order status. We’ll also send you notification emails whenever an item ships or is ready to pick up.

    • When can I pick up my order?

      We’ll email you when your items can be picked up. In-stock items are typically ready within an hour. In some instances, you may be required to select a time window to pick up your order. This will ensure your order is ready shortly after you arrive. Simply check in with a specialist. If you’re unable to come in during this time window, you can still pick up your order, but wait times may vary. Learn more about pickup.

    • When will I get my items?

      We grouped your items into shipments above, and your delivery date appears next to each shipment. You’ll receive notification emails as your items leave our warehouse or become available for pickup at an Apple Store. Learn more about Delivery & Pickup.

    • Can I make changes to my order?

      Visit online Order Status to view or change your order. You can also reach Apple Store Customer Service at 1-800-MY-APPLE. We’re happy to help.

    • What if I will not be available to receive my shipment?

      Apple offers several easy options for you to receive your shipment, including pre-signing for the package; signing with the carrier to release your shipment to your address, your neighbor, or your building manager; or picking up the package at the carrier’s local office. Learn more about Delivery Options.

    • How do I track my shipment?

      Once your item ships from our warehouse, we’ll send you a Shipment Notification email that includes the carrier name and tracking number. Click the link to view the status of your package on the carrier’s website.

      If you ordered multiple items, you may receive separate shipments with no additional shipping charges.

    Questions

    • How do I complete the iPhone trade-in process?

      If you’re upgrading at an Apple Store, bring your current iPhone with you and a Specialist will help you complete your trade in. If you’re having your new iPhone delivered, you’ll receive a Trade-in Kit and a prepaid shipping label so you can easily send us your previous iPhone. The Trade-in Kit includes instructions explaining what you need to do before you send your iPhone back. You’ll have 14 days from the delivery of your new iPhone to send your previous iPhone back.

    • If I upgrade to a new iPhone, what happens to my existing iPhone Upgrade Program monthly payments?

      For shipped orders, the monthly payments for your existing iPhone Upgrade Program will pause once your new iPhone ships. Once you send us your previous iPhone, and if it matches the trade-in criteria, we’ll close the associated loan. If you don’t send your iPhone within 14 days of receiving the Trade-in Kit, the remaining payments will resume — on top of the monthly payments on your new iPhone.

      For pickup orders, your existing monthly payments will pause once your new iPhone is set aside. If you don’t pick up your new iPhone within 14 days, your order will be canceled and your monthly payments will resume.

    • What if the iPhone I’m trading in is damaged?

      If your iPhone requires repairs due to accidental damage, you’ll be asked to pay a repair service fee based on the type of damage. If you decline the repair service fee or have used your allotted AppleCare+ claims on that iPhone, we’ll be unable to complete the repairs and will give the iPhone back to you. Your monthly payments for that iPhone will resume, in addition to the monthly payments for your new iPhone. For questions about AppleCare+ and repair service fees, visit AppleCare+ for iPhone.

    • When I upgrade to a new iPhone with the iPhone Upgrade Program, what happens to my existing AppleCare+ coverage?

      When you complete the iPhone trade-in process, we’ll cancel the associated AppleCare+ coverage. Your new AppleCare+ coverage will be registered to your new iPhone starting on the day you set it up.

    • When will I get my items?

      For shipped orders, your Order Acknowledgement email will display a delivery date, and the Shipment Notification email will display your tracking number. For pickup orders, your new iPhone will be available during the pickup time listed in your Order Acknowledgement email.

    • How do I track my order if it’s being delivered?

      To track your order, click the tracking number in your Shipment Notification email, or click the Track Shipment button above. For more details, refer to the Shipping & Pickup help page.

    • Do I have to be available when my package is delivered?

      To ensure safe delivery, our couriers require a signature. If you’re unable to provide a signature, click “Pre-sign for this shipment” above to sign for your package in advance. For more details, refer to the Shipping & Pickup help page.

    • How do I cancel or edit my order?

      To cancel, click Cancel Item. If you’re canceling an item shipping to an address, you will not be billed. Once you submit your cancel request, we initiate your refund immediately. If your order is eligible, you can edit your gift message, engraving, or ship-to address. For more details, visit the Viewing & Changing Orders help page.

    • How can I return an item?

      If you’d like to return an item, click Return Items to ship the item back to us, or take your item to any U.S. Apple Store. Once we receive your returned item, we’ll initiate your refund immediately. For more details, refer to the Returns & Refunds help page.

    • When will I receive text notifications?

      In addition to email notifications, we will also send you text messages when your items have shipped. Texts are sent between 8:00 a.m. and 9:00 p.m. in your time zone.

    • How do I change my text message preferences?

      You can edit your mobile phone number at any time by signing in to your account and editing the Shipment or Pickup Notification preferences for your order. To stop notifications, reply to the text message with STOP.

    • What if I will not be available to receive my shipment?

      Apple offers several easy options for you to receive your shipment, including pre-signing for the package; signing with the carrier to release your shipment to your address, your neighbor, or your building manager; or picking up the package at the carrier’s local office. Learn more about Delivery Options.

    • How do I ship to an APO/FPO address?

      Apple can ship to APO/FPO addresses using a third-party freight forwarding service, which will conveniently redirect Apple Store shipments to APO/FPO addresses for a fee. Learn more about APO/FPO.

    • Which payment methods can I use?

      If you’re buying a new iPhone through the iPhone Upgrade Program, you must use a credit card and qualify for financing through Citizens One.

      If you’re not buying through the iPhone Upgrade Program, you can use any method of payment presented by the Apple Store during checkout. Please note that PayPal, Apple Store Gift Cards, and financing options may not be available for all products. Some customers may qualify for other forms of payment — call 1-800-MY-APPLE for more information. Learn more about Payment & Pricing (opens in a new window).

    • If I purchase a new iPhone with the iPhone Upgrade Program, can I upgrade early?

      Yes. You can upgrade early by making your first 12 monthly payments in advance. Your new iPhone must be enrolled in the iPhone Upgrade Program for at least six months before you can upgrade.

    • How is sales tax determined for my order?

      The tax listed on the Bag and Checkout pages is an estimate. Your invoice will reflect the final total tax, which includes state taxes, local taxes, and any applicable fees. For the iPhone Upgrade Program, taxes are paid in full at the beginning of an upgrade cycle and are calculated based on the full price of the new iPhone including AppleCare+. Learn more about Payment & Pricing (opens in a new window).

    Order Questions

    • When will I hear from Citizens One?

      Within the next 24 hours, Citizens One will contact the phone number in your Extended Fraud Alert file in order to verify your identity. Once your identity is verified, Apple will process your order. If your identity is not verified, you’ll be notified immediately with next steps.

    • How do I view or change my order?

      Visit your online Order Status at any time to make changes and view the most up-to-date status of your order. You can also call us at
      1-800-MY-APPLE, seven days a week from 7:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m. Central time. Please have your Order Number ready when you call.
